I am a lifelong learner with an innate drive to broaden my design vocabulary. I design by building resolution in thin layers or lifts through iterations of gathering and synthesizing—pulling strings at orchestrated points along the design timeline. The broader the skillsets, the more multi-dimensional the composition.

Work

Industries blur as technologies hasten. With the spatial web/web 3.0/Meta saturating the early adoptors and financial ecosystems shifting to a blockchain model, there is compound value in having a broad toolset. We are seeing the convergence of spatial information networks, augmented/ digitally mediated reality, and crypto, and it's really exciting.

I work across many areas and like to open hoods. My experience ranges from generating financial strategies for real estate investors, to designing digital experiences, to the dream of playing in the sandbox of responsive environments, places driven by data to support experience.
